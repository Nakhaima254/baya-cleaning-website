About Baya Solutions

Learn about our story, mission, and the team behind Nairobi's premier cleaning service.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Baya Solutions began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional cleaning services to homes and businesses across Nairobi. What started as a small team with a single van has grown into a trusted company serving hundreds of satisfied clients.

Our name "Baya" represents our commitment to providing Smart, Wet, and Dry cleaning solutions tailored to each client's unique needs.

Our Mission

To enhance the quality of life for our clients by providing reliable, professional cleaning services that transform spaces and promote health and wellbeing.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We strive for perfection in every service we deliver
  • Integrity: We are honest, transparent, and ethical in all our dealings
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our methods and adopt new technologies
  • Sustainability: We use eco-friendly products and practices whenever possible
